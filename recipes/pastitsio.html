Pastitsio

Description

Greek lasagna! Can make it with lamb, pork, beef, or even chorizo. I prefer mostly lamb, with a bit of beef. Yum!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Penne pasta
  • 1 lb Ground Lamb
  • .5 lb Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • olive oil
  • flour
  • butter
  • Milk
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Red wine
  • Bay leaf
  • Allspice
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Steps

Get Started

  1. Preheat the oven to 350*
  2. Boil pasta until just underdone
  3. Chop garlic and onions

Make Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in pan
  2. Add Garlic and Onions and brown until soft
  3. Add Meat and cook through
  4. Add tomatoes, wine, bayleaf, allspice, nutmeg, and salt and pepper
  5. Simmer for 90 minutes

Build Pastitsio

  1. Ask John to make Bechamel out of the dairy and flour
  2. Put drained pasta in the bottom of a 13x9 pan
  3. Cover pasta with meat sauce, leaving a small amount out for snacking
  4. Clover meat sauce with a layer of bechamel.
  5. Place in the oven and bake for one hour.
