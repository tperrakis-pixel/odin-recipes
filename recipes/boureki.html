Boureki

Description

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Potatoes
  • Mint
  • Dill
  • olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350*
  2. Dill, mint, and feta in a bowl
  3. Slice Potatoes and Zucchini into thin rounds
  4. Create a thin layer of potatoes to cover the bottom of a 13x9 pan
  5. Add a layer of zucchini in the same way
  6. Put half of the feta mixture next
  7. Repeat all three laters one more time
  8. Top with one more layer of potatoes
  9. Place in the oven and bake for one hour
