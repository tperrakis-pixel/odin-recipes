Spanikopita

Description

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Mint
  • Dill
  • Phyllo Dough
  • Feta Cheese
  • two Eggs
  • Butter
  • olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350*
  2. Mix spinach, eggs, dill, mint, and feta in a bowl
  3. Lay out phyllo dough on the counter
  4. Place five pieces of phyllo douch into 13x9 pan, with melted butter spread between each piece
  5. Add half of the spinach mixture on top
  6. Place five more pieces of phyllo dough, spread with butter, on top of spinach mixture
  7. Add the rest of the spinach mixture
  8. Top with three more sheets of phyllo dough and butter
  9. Place in the oven and bake for one hour
